🌱 Life in Weeks

🔗 Live Demo: https://life-in-weeks-vip.vercel.app

A simple yet powerful visual tool that shows your entire life as a grid of weeks — helping you understand how much time you've lived, how much remains, and most importantly, how you spend it.

✨ What This App Does
📊 Visualizes your entire life in weeks
⏳ Shows:
Weeks lived
Weeks remaining
Percentage of life completed
🎯 Highlights important life milestones
🫂 Calculates time left with your parents
🇮🇳 Adds an Indian perspective (Diwali, mango seasons, etc.)
📱 Breaks down how your remaining time is spent (sleep, work, phone, etc.)
💭 Gives thought-provoking life messages
🧠 Idea Behind This

Most people think they have “a lot of time.”

This app makes time:

Visible
Finite
Real

When you see your life as dots, you start asking:

"Is this worth one of my weeks?"

🚀 Features
🔹 1. Life Grid
Each dot = 1 week
Each row = 1 year
Shows:
✅ Past (lived)
🔵 Present (current week)
⚪ Future
🔹 2. Milestones

Predefined key moments:

🎂 First birthday
🎓 Graduation
🪔 Turning 30
🌟 50 years
…and more
🔹 3. Parents Time Calculator 🫂
Estimates:
How many times you'll see your parents again
Total hours left together
One of the most impactful parts of the app
🔹 4. Time Breakdown ⏳

Where your remaining life goes:

😴 Sleep
💼 Work
📱 Phone usage
🫂 Family
✨ Free time
🔹 5. Indian Life Counters 🇮🇳

A culturally relatable perspective:

🪔 Diwalis left
🥭 Mango seasons
🌧️ Monsoons
☕ Sunday mornings
🔹 6. Personalized Insights 💭

Dynamic messages based on your age and remaining time.

🛠️ Tech Stack
⚛️ React (Hooks)
🎨 Pure CSS
🚀 Deployed on Vercel
❌ No backend
❌ No APIs
🔒 100% privacy (data stays in browser)
